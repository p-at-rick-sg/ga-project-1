Game Setup

Select Easy (4x4 Grid), Medium (10x10 Grid) or Hard (20x20 Grid)

You can override the default timers to make the game more or less challenging - this will reflect in your score if you win.

Game Play

Your task is to clear all cells. Right click marks a cell if you believe it to be a mine. Left click 'clears' that cell. If it is not a mine, if will reveal a number which is the total number of mines in the adjacent cells.

If there are no mines in the adjacent cells, then your helper minesweeper bot will clear the adjacent cells until there is a mine encountered and update the cells accordingly

You win when all mines are correctly marked and all other cells are successfully cleared.

Good Luck!
